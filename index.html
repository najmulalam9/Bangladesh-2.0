Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

The pillars of Bangladesh's innovation are smart citizens, smart government, smart economy, and smart society. Smart citizens are digitally empowered individuals who drive innovation through their knowledge and skills. A smart government provides an efficient and supportive environment for innovation to flourish. A smart economy is driven by knowledge and technology, fostering a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship.

Sustainability

Sustainability serves as a cornerstone for Bangladesh's progress, ensuring a balance between economic growth, social development, and environmental protection. 1 It guides the nation towards a future where resources are utilized responsibly, ecological integrity is maintained, and all citizens have the opportunity to thrive.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the backbone of Bangladesh's development, facilitating economic growth and improving the quality of life for its citizens. 1 A robust infrastructure network, encompassing transportation, energy, telecommunications, and other essential services, is crucial for connecting people, businesses, and markets, unlocking the country's full potential.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the bedrock of Bangladesh's progress, fostering a society where all individuals, regardless of background, have the opportunity to reach their full potential. By empowering its citizens through education, access to resources, and equal opportunities, Bangladesh unlocks its human potential and drives sustainable development.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
